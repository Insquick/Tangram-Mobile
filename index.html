THE ART OF SPATIAL REASONING

Founded in 2015, Lux Riddles emerged from a passion for challenging the mind through tangible beauty. Our puzzles are more than mere objects - they are conversations between form and function.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted from premium materials including hand-polished hardwoods, brushed metals, and in some cases, semi-precious stones. The manufacturing process can take up to three months for a single puzzle.

We collaborate with world-renowned designers and craftsmen to create puzzles that are as visually striking as they are intellectually stimulating. The result is a collection that challenges perception while elevating any space it occupies.
